Discover American Sign Language

American Sign Language (ASL) is a vibrant, expressive language used by millions in the United States and parts of Canada. ASL has its own grammar, culture, and community, providing a rich communication system for Deaf and Hard of Hearing individuals.

ASL and AI

With the development of AI, ASL has become easier to access and AI has been used to assist in translation, learning, and cultural preservation. Check out the graphic below for more information on how AI has been used for ASL language translation.

Education & Learning

ASL education is available in schools, universities, and online platforms, empowering learners to communicate effectively and connect with the Deaf community.

  • ASL classes in K-12 and higher education
  • Workshops and community programs
  • Online ASL learning resources ex. Lingvano, ASLDeafined

Why I Love ASL

ASL is such a beautiful language and I think that everyone should learn it. For hearing people, it can be very useful in multiple situations where you either can't talk or can't hear others. Learning ASL also opens doors for you to learn about the deaf committee and its extremely rich culture.
